Why Mental Health Matters in Women's Sports

Mental health is just as important as physical health. Female athletes often face pressure from competition, academics, family expectations, and social media. Recognizing these challenges is the first step toward building resilience.

Recognizing Burnout

Burnout can include constant fatigue, loss of motivation, poor performance, and emotional exhaustion. Early support can prevent long-term issues.
